# ✈️ High Cloud Airlines Performance Dashboard

## 🎯 Problem Statement

High Cloud Airlines has been experiencing increased flight delays and rising customer dissatisfaction. Operational inefficiencies, inconsistent on-time performance, and frequent cancellations are impacting overall service quality and profitability.

The management team requires a data-driven dashboard to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify delay patterns, track cancellation trends, and evaluate route-level performance. The objective is to enable data-backed decision-making to improve operational efficiency, enhance customer satisfaction, and optimize airline performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The main objectives of this project are:

- Monitor overall airline operational performance  
- Analyze on-time performance and delay trends  
- Identify high-delay routes and airports  
- Track cancellation rates and monthly trends  
- Provide actionable insights for performance improvement  

---

## 📊 Project Approach

### 🔹 Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Removed missing and inconsistent records  
- Standardized date and time formats  
- Created calculated columns:
  - Delay Duration  
  - On-Time Status Flag  
  - Cancellation Indicator  
  - Month & Year extraction  

### 🔹 KPI Development
Developed key performance indicators such as:

- Total Flights  
- On-Time Performance %  
- Average Arrival Delay  
- Average Departure Delay  
- Cancellation Rate  
- Flights by Route  
- Flights by Airport  
- Monthly Performance Trends  

### 🔹 Dashboard Design
- Interactive filters (Month, Route, Airport)  
- KPI summary cards for quick insights  
- Delay distribution analysis  
- Route-level performance comparison  
- Cancellation trend visualization  
- Executive-friendly clean layout  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Certain routes consistently show higher delay durations.  
- Peak travel months have increased cancellation rates.  
- Morning flights demonstrate better on-time performance.  
- A small number of airports contribute significantly to overall delays.  

---

## 💼 Business Impact

This dashboard helps airline management to:

- Identify operational bottlenecks  
- Improve scheduling strategies  
- Reduce delays and cancellations  
- Enhance customer satisfaction  
- Enable data-driven strategic decisions
